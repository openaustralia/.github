The OpenAustralia Foundation is an Australian charity that builds public digital infrastructure for democracy. Our mission is to give all Australians the tools they need to effect the change they want.

We are strictly non-partisan. We are not the government, nor are we affiliated with any political party. Everything we build is open source, and you'll find it all here.

## Our services

| Service | What it does | Source code |
|---|---|---|
| [Planning Alerts](https://www.planningalerts.org.au) | Emails you details of planning applications near you, so you know what's changing in your community | [planningalerts](https://github.com/openaustralia/planningalerts) |
| [Right to Know](https://www.righttoknow.org.au) | Helps you make Freedom of Information requests to Australian governments, and publishes the responses for everyone | [righttoknow](https://github.com/openaustralia/righttoknow) |
| [They Vote for You](https://theyvoteforyou.org.au) | Shows you how your Member of Parliament votes on the issues that matter to you | [theyvoteforyou](https://github.com/openaustralia/theyvoteforyou) |
| [OpenAustralia.org.au](https://www.openaustralia.org.au) | Makes it easy to read, search and follow what's said in the Australian Parliament | [openaustralia](https://github.com/openaustralia/openaustralia) |
| [morph.io](https://morph.io) | Runs and hosts web scrapers, making public data easier for everyone to use | [morph](https://github.com/openaustralia/morph) |

## Contributing

We're a small team, so contributions make a real difference. You don't need to be a developer to help - people contribute scrapers, research, documentation and bug reports too.

A good place to start is the open issues on the repository of the service you use. Some projects ask contributors to sign our [Contributor Licence Agreement](https://github.com/openaustralia/.github/blob/main/CLA/CLA.md).

## Support our work

Our services run on donations from people who use them and want them to keep going. You can [donate at donate.oaf.org.au](https://donate.oaf.org.au).
